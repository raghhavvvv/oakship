WHO WE ARE

Established in 2005, OAK Shipping Services Pvt Ltd has been a trusted leader in the logistics sector for almost two decades. OSSPL is specialized in tailored freight forwarding solutions, managing everything from customs clearance to last-mile delivery. With our dedicated team of experts, we ensure seamless transportation of goods globally. Our commitment to efficiency, reliability & customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry. Whether by air, sea, or land, we deliver on time, making global trade hassle-free. Choose us for unparalleled service quality in freight forwarding. No matter the size of your business, rely on us to handle your freight with meticulous care and maximum efficiency.

OUR SERVICES

At OSSPL ,we offer a full spectrum of logistics services. We leverage our extensive network and expertise to provide tailored solutions that meet your specific needs, ensuring timely delivery and cost-effectiveness. Trust us to handle your freight with precision and care, every step of the way.

AIR
Experience swift import and export solutions with OSSPL air freight services. Leveraging our global network and cutting-edge tracking technology, we ensure timely delivery of your cargo worldwide.
SEA
Optimize your import and export processes through our sea freight services. Utilize our broad network of partnerships and deep knowledge in maritime logistics to secure efficient, economical, and dependable Ocean transportation solutions.
LAND
Enhance your supply chain with our versatile land transportation services, including trucking, rail, and intermodal transport, delivering efficient solutions customized to your needs.
WAREHOUSING
Discover streamlined storage with our warehousing services. Our top-tier facilities ensure goods' safety, with flexible options tailored to your needs. Manage inventory seamlessly with advanced systems and enhance operations with value-added services
CUSTOMS BROKERAGE
Experience streamlined customs solutions with our services. Our expert team ensures smooth clearance for your shipments, simplifying procedures and minimizing delays. Trust us for hassle-free imports and exports.
